# <font size=6>Welcome to Yin Feng's homepage!</font>

My research interests focus on transportation systems, traffic control, electric vehicle charging infrastructure, optimization, machine learning, and driver behavior modeling.

# Research Experience

### Speed Optimization Control Strategy for Autonomous Vehicles in Highway Tunnel Areas Based on Hazard Perception

*Project Leader | Xi'an, China | 2024*

- Developed a speed optimization control strategy for autonomous vehicles in highway tunnel areas under mixed traffic conditions.
- Used millimeter-wave radar data and Seq2Seq neural networks to predict vehicle speeds near tunnel entrances and exits.
- Integrated speed prediction and lane-change timing prediction to reduce rear-end collision risk and improve driving safety.

### Signal Timing Optimization for Urban Intersection Groups Based on Markov Prediction and Driver Characteristics

*Key Project Member | Xi'an, China | 2023*

- Developed a coordinated signal timing optimization framework for urban intersection groups under increasing traffic demand.
- Incorporated Markov prediction and driver behavior characteristics to estimate vehicle trajectories between intersections.
- Used video-based traffic flow analysis to support route-choice prediction and adaptive signal timing optimization.
